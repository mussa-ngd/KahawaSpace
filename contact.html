Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click "Join" in the top right corner and fill in your details. It's free and takes less than a minute.
How does shipping work?
Shipping costs and timelines are specified on each product listing. You'll receive tracking info when your order ships.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.
Can I return coffee?
Due to the perishable nature of coffee, we don't accept returns, but contact us if you have any issues.
