1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our platform, and improve your overall experience. They do not contain personal information unless you have provided it to us.

2. How We Use Cookies

Kahawa Space uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly (login, shopping cart, checkout).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences (language, currency, region).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our platform and improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.
  • Security Cookies: Help protect your account and detect suspicious activity.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services that may place cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze site traffic and user behavior.
  • Facebook Pixel: Helps us measure advertising effectiveness.
  • Stripe/Payment Processors: Securely process payments.
  • Intercom/Live Chat: Provides customer support functionality.

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We recommend reviewing them for more information.

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View and delete cookies stored on your device
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

To learn how to manage cookies in your browser, please refer to your browser's help documentation:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
