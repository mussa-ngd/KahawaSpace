The Journey Begins

Kahawa Space was founded in 2024 by a group of coffee lovers who saw an opportunity to create a platform that celebrates coffee culture and connects people with exceptional beans from around the world.

Our name "Kahawa" comes from the Swahili word for coffee, honoring the rich coffee heritage of East Africa where our journey began. We believe that coffee has the power to bring people together.
